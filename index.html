MUTTON BIRYANI

This recipe will help you make the most AMAZING Mutton Biryani. With this recipe, nail that 3-ingredient marinade for the mutton, learn how to make the mutton masala for biryani, and how to get the biryani layers right! INGREDIENTS (1 CUP=240)

Marination

  • 1/2 kg mutton (cut to any size)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (up to 1tsp)
  • 1 tsp biryani masala powder or garam masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cub curd
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsps raw papaya paste
    •

    HOW TO MAKE MUTTON BIRYANI

    Preparation

    • marinate mutton with chili powder,biryani masala,yogurt,ginger,turmeric,lemon juice,oil and salt
    • refrigerate for at least 4 hours.overnight is best.
    • if you are using raw papaya paste,you can just set aside for 1 hour & begin to prepare.
    • wash rice and soak for 30 to 40 minutes.
    • drain it completely to a colander and set aside.
    • Heat a heavy bottom pa or a pressure cooker with oil.add the whole spices.
    • Add the rest of the half cup curd to the cooked mutton
    • next add few chopped coriander and mint leaves.
    • few fried onions .mix everything well.
    • you can check the salt and spice at this stage and adjust
    • layer the rice evenly over the cooked lamb meat.
    • spinkle some chopped coriander,mint leaves,fried onions and tbsp of ghee.
    • lastly pour the saffron milk.
    • cover the rim of the biryani pot with a moist thick clean cloth or foil.
    • place a heavy lid over it.IF you do not have a heavy lid.you can seal it with atta.
    • Heat a old tawa until very hot.place this biryani pot over hot the tawa and cook on a low flame for 15 mins.
    • allow to rest for at least 15 mins.
    • serve mutton biryani with raita
